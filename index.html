About Me

I am currently a PhD student in the Mathematical Physics group at the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Tim Adamo (you can find a list of his publications here). I did my undergraduate in physics and a masters in theoretical physics at the University of Oxford. I completed courses on Representation Theory, Quantum Field Theory, Supersymmetry, String Theory, Conformal Field Theory and Topological Quantum Theory. I also wrote a dissertation on the novel topic of higher generalised symmetries, under the supervision of Dr. Andrei Constantin. Linked here is my updated CV.
